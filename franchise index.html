The World of Jurassic Park

The Jurassic Park franchise began with Michael Crichton's bestselling novel in 1990, which quickly caught the attention of director Steven Spielberg. In 1993, the world was introduced to Jurassic Park, a theme park where dinosaurs roam once again. The films success sparked a series of sequels, bringing audiences closer to dinosaurs with every installment. The franchise has since expanded into multiple films, animated series, and video games, becoming a cultural phenomenon that has captivated fans of all ages. The iconic dinosaurs, thrilling adventures, and groundbreaking effects have cemented Jurassic Park's place in cinema history.

Main Franchise Movies

Jurassic Park

The original film that started it all! Jurassic Park takes us to Isla Nublar, where scientists have resurrected dinosaurs and created a theme park. But when the parks security system fails, visitors are plunged into a terrifying struggle for survival. Directed by Steven Spielberg, this film introduced audiences to iconic dinosaurs like the T-Rex and Velociraptors.

The Lost World: Jurassic Park

In the thrilling sequel, we return to a different island where dinosaurs roam freely. Dr. Ian Malcolm (Jeff Goldblum) and a team venture to Isla Sorna, only to find that nature has taken over. With more action and new species of dinosaurs, this movie explores the dangers of unchecked scientific ambition.

Jurassic Park III

Dr. Alan Grant (Sam Neill) is lured back to Isla Sorna under false pretenses in this action-packed installment. A rescue mission goes wrong, leading to encounters with deadly new dinosaurs, including the massive Spinosaurus. This film takes a survival-focused approach, highlighting the unpredictability of these ancient creatures.

Jurassic World

In a modernized park built on the original site, visitors are thrilled by genetically engineered dinosaurs until an experimental hybrid, the Indominus Rex, escapes. Jurassic World brings new thrills and challenges the ethics of genetic manipulation, with Owen Grady (Chris Pratt) and Claire Dearing (Bryce Dallas Howard) at the helm.

Spinoffs & Extended Universe

Jurassic World: Camp Cretaceous

This animated series follows a group of teens attending a camp on Isla Nublar, who must survive on their own when the dinosaurs break loose. Camp Cretaceous explores new stories in the Jurassic World universe and brings fresh perspectives on the dinosaurs we know and love.

Other Spinoffs

Additional projects in development aim to expand the Jurassic universe, with more thrilling encounters and perspectives that allow fans to experience new sides of this prehistoric world.

Books, Games, & Beyond

Jurassic Park Novel by Michael Crichton

The novel that inspired it all! Michael Crichton's Jurassic Park dives deeper into the science and ethical dilemmas of cloning dinosaurs. The book remains a must-read for fans of the franchise, with its suspenseful storytelling and complex characters.

Jurassic World Evolution (Video Game)

Take control and build your own dinosaur park! Jurassic World Evolution allows players to manage and maintain their own Jurassic World, making critical decisions about dinosaur genetics, park layout, and safety. This game brings the excitement of running a dinosaur theme park to life.
