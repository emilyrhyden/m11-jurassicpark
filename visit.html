Embark on Your Jurassic Adventure

Jurassic Park at Universal's Islands of Adventure in Orlando immerses visitors in a world where dinosaurs roam the Earth once again. This area of the park brings Michael Crichton's iconic novel and Steven Spielberg's blockbuster film to life, with lush, prehistoric-inspired landscapes filled with life-size dinosaur replicas, interactive exhibits, and thrilling rides. The main attraction here is Jurassic Park River Adventure, a water-based ride that takes guests on a boat journey through a Jurassic-themed island, only to encounter the terrifying T. rex in a heart-pounding drop. Another fan favorite is Pteranodon Flyers, a family-friendly ride where guests soar above the treetops, offering a bird's-eye view of the Jurassic Park area. The immersive setting of Jurassic Park is also enhanced by dining options like the Thunder Falls Terrace, where guests can enjoy a meal surrounded by scenic waterfalls and dinosaur-themed décor.

Main Park Attractions

Jurassic Park River Adventure

One minute, you're gliding along in your raft. The next, you're face to face with a giant T. rex. There's nowhere to turn. She's bearing down on you and your only escape from her razor sharp teeth is to take the pitch-black plunge before you. Did we mention it's an 85-foot drop?

Jurassic World VelociCoaster

Speeding through the jungle, rising high above the terrain, it's the apex predator of roller coasters: the Jurassic World VelociCoaster. Board your vehicle for a high-speed dash through the raptor paddock. Feel the rush of the hunt as you race these nimble predators soaring above the land and water.

Ride Details

Pteranodon Gliding Flyers

An easy air glide, just for kids. Taking flight beneath the 10-foot wings of a Pteranodon, little adventurers will get an amazing view of all the theme-park fun. Slowly soar & gently swing through the air in a comfy seat suspended. This ride is perfect for younger guests.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Where can I buy my tickets?

At any Universal Studios licensed provider or from the main Universal Ticket Site

Can I ride Dinosaurs there at Jurassic Park?

Unfortunatly, no. Our Dinosaurs do not like people riding them. However they are happy to engage with you in the rides or in the other experiences available in the park.

Are these real Dinosaurs?

No, since Dinosaurs have been extinct for over 50 Million Years. But we love to pretend!
