Famous Dinosaurs

In every Jurassic Park and Jurassic World movie, there are iconic dinosaurs that shape the movie. Not only are their human antagonists, but also some very interesting, genetically created jurassic creatures to follow. Normally failed genetic combinations, these dinosaurs have become a staple of the Jurassic Park series. Let's take a quick look at the top 12 of them that you might see in visiting Jurassic Park.

Also known as International Genetics Incorporated, International Genetic Technologies, The InGen Corporation, InGen was founded by John Hammond. The company performed research to recreate animals that have been extinct for thousands of years. Their aim at the end of the day was to close those creatures and exploit them using none other than a theme park called Jurassic Park and Jurassic World. In 1998, InGen became a subsidiary of Masrani Global Corporation's InGen Technologies. Two other genetic companies, though separate, but featured in the movies are Mantah Corp and Biosyn.

One of the constants within all those companies is Dr. Henry Wu. He is the chief genetic engineer of InGen and the one who recreates the dinosaurs in all of the Jurassic Park series. In Jurassic World: Dominion, he was known for his error of causing the Agrarian Crisis of the Locusts while working for Biosyn. He was an antagonist all up until close to the end. With the locust crisis, he ended up switching sides to help stop the downfall of the world from the giant locusts.
